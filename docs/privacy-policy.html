Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Overview

Autotask Helper ("the Extension") is designed to improve productivity within the Autotask platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any user data.

  • No Personal Information: The Extension does not ask for or access personal information.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing history or usage patterns.
  • No Analytics: The Extension does not include any analytics software or third-party tracking scripts.
  • No Server: The Extension operates entirely locally on your device. It does not communicate with any external servers (other than the Autotask host pages you visit, which it modifies strictly for the purpose of the provided functionality).

Permissions Usage

The Extension requests specific permissions solely to function:

  • Host Permissions (`*.autotask.net`): To run scripts and detect URLs only on Autotask pages.
  • Clipboard: To copy cleaned URLs to your clipboard.
  • Storage: To save your local preferences (e.g., toggling features on/off).

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support tab.
